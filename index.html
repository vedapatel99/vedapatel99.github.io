Plant Disease Image Classification with Neural Networks

In this group project, we developed neural network models for tomatoes and potatoes to classify plants as healthy or affected by blight. Subreddit Classification with NLP

In this project, I developed an NLP model to analyze language differences between r/AskWomen and r/AskMen, uncovering gender-based variations in questions and topics. Using a stacked model with multiple classifiers, I achieved a 62.2% test accuracy, revealing the complexity of gender dynamics in today's world.
